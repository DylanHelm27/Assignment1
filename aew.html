About Us

AEW, or All Elite Wrestling, is seen as the cloest modern day equivalent to a challenger brand in the world of pro wrestling. Created in 2019 by the Elite (Cody Rhodes, Kenny Omega, Matt & Nick Jackson) and bankrolled by the son of billionare Shad Khan. Priding itself with the title "Where the Best Wrestle" moniker, it focus more on the athleticism of the athletes, as opposed to the storytelling from the WWE.

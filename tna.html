About Us

TNA, or Total Nonstop Action (formerly called Impact Wrestling) was founded in 2002 by former wrestlers Jeff and Jerry Jarrett as an alternative to WWE. It stood out with its six-sided ring and fast-paced X-Division matches, featuring stars like AJ Styles, Samoa Joe, and former WWE talent such as Christian and Kurt Angle. The company gained attention for its unique matches like Ultimate X. Over the years, it has faced ups and downs but remains active today, with a dedicated fanbase.

About Us

WWE, or World Wrestling Entertainment, is a professional wrestling company that combines athletic competition with dramatic storylines and entertainment. It started in 1953 and has become a global phenomenon with shows like Raw and SmackDown and major events like WrestleMania. WWE is known for its larger-than-life wrestlers, exciting rivalries, and high-energy matches. Beyond wrestling, it has expanded into merchandise, streaming platforms, and live tours, building a huge fan base around the world.
